Felt

Concept/inspiration

For my midwifery university application, I had to prepare a presentation. We were not allowed to use a digital format, but we could use any other medium to support the presentation. I chose to make a flipchart out of felt, using different felt graphics to visualise and present my information.

Materials and techniques

Felt graphic 2023

Challenges/discoveries

Designing the graphics and finding the right colours was a challenge, as was transferring my patterns onto felt and cutting out the small details. Arranging the pieces like a puzzle and glueing them onto the flipchart also required careful planning so that everything made sense with my presentation and clearly visualised what I was talking about.

Crochet

Concept/inspiration

Created my own crochet pattern for a kiwi bird. The first design is in green, the second in black with a silver fern to represent the All Blacks, and the third is split in half to reveal a kiwifruit design.

Materials and techniques

Crochet 2023

Challenges/discoveries

I created my own crochet pattern through trial and error, experimenting with different designs and variations. Splitting the kiwi in half required adjusting the pattern to fit the new design. I wrote the pattern down and had others test it online, using their feedback to refine the final version.

Collaborative effort

I created and wrote the pattern, then I found a small group of people willing to help me test it. I guided them through any issues and incorporated their feedback into the final pattern.

Concept/inspiration

In the Austrian Alps, there is a tradition called “Almabtrieb” (cattle drive). The cows spend all summer up in the mountains, but for winter, they are brought down so they won’t run out of food. It is a yearly tradition to dress the cows up with decorative headdresses and flowers while bringing them down from the mountains. The event is celebrated as a town festival with traditional food and drinks.

Materials and techniques

Crochet 2022

Challenges/discoveries

Experimenting with different types of wool to create the cow and headdresses. Figuring out how to make the headdress stable enough to stand upright, I ended up crocheting it around a dark green pipe cleaner for support.

Concept/inspiration

A wedding present for my cousin and his wife

Materials and techniques

Crochet 2023

Challenges/discoveries

I created my own pattern to make the figures resemble real people. From previous projects, I learned that using foam hair rollers in the legs and neck gives the figures strength, allowing them to stand upright and preventing floppy necks. I incorporated magnets into the mouths and hands so the figures could kiss and hold hands. Originally, I considered snaps or velcro, but these were too visually distracting, so magnets were a better hidden solution.

Concept/inspiration

Halloween crochet skeleton

Materials and techniques

Crochet 2025 – in progress

Challenges/discoveries

Working from a crochet pattern, I used much thicker wool than the creator intended, which makes my skeleton life-sized. Figuring out the right amount of wire for the internal frame was tricky. Crocheting around the stiff wire so the figure could stand and support its weight is challenging, and assembling all the parts required careful sewing in hard-to-reach areas.
