Portfolio Lia Clement
Concrete work

Concept/inspiration

My parents' entryway needed to be redone, and I was able to get creative with it

Materials and techniques

Concrete 2025 – ongoing project

Challenges/discoveries

I had to create a concept design that balanced my parents’ very different visions for their new entryway, combining their ideas into a cohesive design. The first batch of turtles had a lot of air bubbles in the concrete, so we had to figure out how to remove the trapped air before the concrete set.

Collaborative effort

I created the concept design and painted the concrete turtles. I worked with my dad to make the concrete turtles using a wire framework. This project is still in progress: The next stage of embedding the turtles and colourful stones into the new concrete entryway and ensuring everything is level and safe will be a combined effort involving the whole family.
